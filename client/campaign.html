Terms/Definitions

Contact You've identified the person you are trying to speak with and received a response to the first question on your script
Ids You've identified and marked their type of response to the first question [Yes Id = Support, Undecided = Unsure, No = Oppose, Refused = Refusal]
Opt in [text/email] You've identified and marked that the person would like to stay in touch via text/email
Volunteer You've identified and marked that the person would like to commit to volunteer

Tips for Success

Treat every call as if its your first! Even though you may have made hundreds of calls today, remember that its the first call for the person we are calling. Keep your energy high and stay engaging. Step away for a moment if you need a quick break.

Make the Script your own! The script is a skeleton. You are what makes the script come alive! Add your own personality and flair. It makes a huge difference.

Make Connections! Get to know the people you are calling, stay personable. Making a lasting connection, however short, can leave a great impression on the other line.

Reach out for help! If you are engaging in a difficult call or tough conversation, reach out to your team lead for support. We are here to help you succeed!
